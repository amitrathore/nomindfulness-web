## Overview
Nomindfulness is an ultra-exclusive mountain meditation retreat — a portal for high-intellect, high-achieving individuals who have mastered the external world but seek the final frontier: direct self-realization. The brand communicates supreme confidence, intellectual rigor, and rare access. The aesthetic is spare, deliberate, and deeply premium — like a Zen monastery designed by Aman.

## Color usage
- **Background #F8F5F0**: The primary canvas — warm cream, never harsh white. Every page breathes on this ground.
- **Primary #1A1814**: Near-black with warmth for all primary text and the logo. Never pure #000000.
- **Secondary #6B6560**: Warm mid-gray for body copy, captions, supporting text.
- **Accent #8B7355**: A refined bronze-earth tone — used ONLY for hairline borders, small decorative elements, the circle in the logo, and select hover states. Never used in large blocks.
- **Neutral #E8E4DF**: The border and divider color. All hairlines use this.
- **Surface #FFFFFF**: Cards, form fields, elevated panels.

## Typography
Cormorant Garamond handles all display and heading work — its literary, refined quality communicates depth and tradition. Weights should range from 300 (light, meditative) to 400 (book). Inter handles all body, UI, labels — clean and modern, zero friction. Never use bold (700+) for display headings — the restraint IS the statement. Uppercase tracking (`tracking-widest`) in Inter for labels, eyebrow text, and nav items communicates structure without weight.

## Layout
Generosity of space is the primary design tool. Sections breathe with 120px+ vertical padding. Content columns should max at 680px for body text. Heroes are full-width. The grid is always centered with generous side margins (120px desktop). Horizontal hairline rules (#E8E4DF, 1px) divide sections instead of color changes. Never stack more than two content elements without whitespace relief.

## Do's and Don'ts
- Do: Let white space carry the luxury. Restraint is the message.
- Do: Use Cormorant Garamond at large, light weights for maximum elegance.
- Do: Use the accent bronze ONLY as a hairline or tiny accent — never fill blocks with it.
- Do: Use all-caps Inter tracking-widest for small section labels/eyebrows.
- Don't: Use gradients anywhere — this brand is flat, pure, material.
- Don't: Use drop shadows — depth comes from spacing and typographic contrast.
- Don't: Use more than 2 columns in body content areas.
- Don't: Use rounded corners larger than 4px — this is angular, precise.
- Don't: Use emojis, icons heavily — restraint everywhere.
- Don't: Use bright colors, high saturation, or gradients.
