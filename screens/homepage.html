The Question

This is not a retreat.
This is a reckoning.

You have read the books. You have attended the seminars. You have meditated, journaled, optimized. Perhaps you have built something remarkable, accumulated what the world calls success. And yet — in the quiet moments between one achievement and the next — there is still that unresolved question.

Nomindfulness exists for those who are ready to stop circling the answer and arrive at it directly.

What Awaits

Three Days. One Arrival.

I

Direct Transmission

Not lectures. Not frameworks. The teacher works with you directly — in silence, in dialogue, in the lived moment of understanding. What has always been obvious becomes unmistakably so.

II

The Mountain Container

A private four-bedroom house in the Sierra Nevada foothills. No groups of strangers. Maximum four participants. Every room, every meal, every silence is calibrated to the work.

III

Intellectual Rigor

This is not devotion or belief. The iRealization method is a precise, verifiable inquiry into the nature of mind and reality — demanding your full intelligence, not its suspension.

"Secrets of the universe,
revealed to the earnest."

The Teacher

Not a guide.
A mirror.

The author of iRealization.com does not teach from study. The transmission comes from direct realization — the kind that restructures how consciousness relates to itself, permanently.

What took decades of sincere inquiry to arrive at has been refined into a method that works for the modern, analytical mind — without the spiritual scaffolding that so often gets in the way.

About the Teacher

A Word of Candor

This retreat is not for everyone.
It is for you, if you are ready.

The work here is demanding. It asks you to bring your full intellectual capacity, your willingness to be wrong about everything you've assumed about yourself, and a genuine hunger for what is actually true — not what is comfortable. Those who are merely curious are gently redirected. Those who are ready are changed.
